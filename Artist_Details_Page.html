Elena Rossi

Born in Florence, Italy, Elena Rossi is an Italian artist currently residing in Rome, Italy. Her artistic practice centers around oil painting, with a focus on capturing the fleeting moments of nature. Elena Rossi's work has been shown at the Biennale di Venezia and several smaller galleries in Italy.
